About Us

At 7 Wonder Decor and Events, we believe every celebration deserves to be unforgettable.

What began as a small passion project has grown into a dream business built on creativity, dedication, and a deep love for decor. From elegant wedding setups to vibrant half saree ceremonies, first birthdays to traditional pooja events — we specialize in bringing your vision to life with style and heart.

We’re all about personalized details, DIY touches, and creating picture-perfect moments that reflect your story and traditions.

With us, it’s not just decoration. It’s emotion, art, and celebration — beautifully blended.

Our Services

  • Half Saree Ceremonies – Traditional and modern backdrops, stage décor, floral jewelry, and themed props.
  • First Birthdays – Custom themes, balloon arches, cake tables, and playful photo zones.
  • Pooja Events – Clean and elegant traditional mandap setups, floral work, and rangoli.
  • Weddings – Engagement to reception décor, aisle styling, drapes, and custom themes.
  • Baby Showers – Soft pastels, floral arrangements, themed centerpieces, and welcoming setups.
  • Engagements – Romantic backdrops, elegant florals, and personalized stage settings.
  • Receptions – Grand lighting, elegant themes, floral chandeliers, and luxurious arrangements.
